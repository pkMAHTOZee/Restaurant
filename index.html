Idli

Idli is a beloved South Indian dish, cherished for its simplicity and wholesome flavor. It is often paired with coconut chutney.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originates from the Indian subcontinent. It features tender pieces of chicken marinated in a blend of spices.

Sambar Rice

Sambar Rice, also known as Sambar Sadam, is a traditional South Indian one-pot meal that combines the flavors of rice, lentils, and vegetables with a spicy and tangy sambar masala

Panner Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer or snack made from cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and grilled to perfection.

Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a beloved street food dish from India, consisting of a spicy vegetable mash (bhaji) served with soft buttered bread rolls (pav).

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a classic Indian dish renowned for its rich and creamy texture and delectable flavors.
